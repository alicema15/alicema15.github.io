Our Philosophy

A new approach to public data

Public data is a powerful tool that we can leverage to hold our public figures and institutions accountable. However, most public data — ranging from government records, to news, to social media — is messy, unstructured, and inaccessible.

Archer is building a community working to make this data accessible and actionable for everyone.
