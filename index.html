A new approach to public data

Public data is the bedrock upon which citizens stay informed about their institutions and their world.

Unstructured data

Most data existing on the internet is unstructured. Large datasets filled with millions of lines of undecipherable text are treasure troves of information. However, these files are near impossible for any human to read and come away with anything more useful than a headache.

Structured data

This level references any data that has been organized in some useful way.

Data visualization

Rather than looking for general patterns across millions of lines of text and numbers, it's much, much easier for someone to glance at a chart and instantly deduce an upward trend.

Drawing insights
